# Gameplay Summary

## Core Loop

1. Open the app to the menu scene.
2. Tap once to stand up.
3. Tap again to start the run.
4. Dodge hazards, collect seeds, survive as long as possible.
5. On death, review score and restart.

## Controls

- Tap: jump
- Hold: higher jump
- Swipe down: duck / slide

## Progression

- Distance increases score and difficulty.
- Seeds fill the bloom meter and also increase lifetime garden currency.
- Garden progression unlocks decorative plant cards with saved progress.
- Best-distance runs are recorded as ghost replays.

## Run Systems

- 5 biome cycle, 500m each
- Score milestones trigger extra feedback
- Mercy hearts accumulate from near misses
- Bloom grants temporary invulnerability

## Current Scope

The shipped implementation is a single-player offline endless runner. The repo does not contain online systems, account systems, cloud save, ads, or in-app purchases.
